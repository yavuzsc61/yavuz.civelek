Profile

Mechanical Design Engineer with hands-on experience in automation and special-purpose machinery across automotive production lines and medical device manufacturing. Experienced in designing complete machine systems from concept through manufacturing and commissioning, with a strong focus on functionality, manufacturability, and system integration.

Based in Ireland, with hands-on experience delivering machine designs from concept through manufacturing and commissioning at manufacturing facilities in Ireland and Turkey, for projects involving Toyota, Renault, Ford, Boston Scientific, DSM, and Medtronic etc.
